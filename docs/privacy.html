Privacy Policy

O'zbekcha

reyeltor.uz helps people find and list property in Tashkent. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you use the app, we may collect and store:

  • Phone number — to create your account and let buyers reach you
  • Email address — for your account, sign-in, and service messages
  • Listing details — property information you choose to publish (price, location, description, and similar fields)
  • Photos — images you upload for your listings
  • Location data — coordinates you place on the map when posting a listing (not continuous background tracking)

How we use your information

We use your data to operate reyeltor.uz — for example, to show listings on the map, manage your account and coin balance, authenticate you, and improve the service.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing. We share data only with service providers needed to run the app (such as hosting and authentication), under appropriate safeguards.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your account and listing data while your account is active. You may request account deletion by contacting us; we will remove or anonymize personal data unless we must retain it for legal reasons.

Children

reyeltor.uz is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email us at privacy@reyeltor.uz.
